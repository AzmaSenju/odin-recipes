Canjero

This is the traditional pancakes of Somalia, similar to Baghrir but this one is slightly fermented.

Firstly we will list all the required ingredients for this recipe:

  • 5 cup of AP flour
  • 1/2 cup sorghum flour
  • 4 cups warm water
  • 1 Tbsp instant dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • olive oil
  • honey
  • melted butter

Instructions

  1. Put all of the ingredients in a blender except for the melted butter and honey and half of the water and the AP flour and blend everything together till you have a consistent batter. Pour into a bowl and let it sit for 1 hour in room temperature.
  2. AFter the hour add the rest of the flour and water and mix till smooth consistence.
  3. Preheat a non-stick pan over medium heat and then add olive oil.
  4. Pour roughly 1/3 cups of the batter in the pan, cover with a lid and let it cook until golden brown, this usually takes 2-3 mins.
  5. Repeat for each canjero and make sure to add a bit of olive oil after every canjero.
  6. Place done canjero on a plate and pour a bit of melted butter.
  7. Serve warm and with honey on the side to drizzle on top if wish to do so.
  8. ENJOY!
