BAGHRIR!! The Moroccan Pancake

This is a recipe of a traditional Moroccan pancake, they are fluffy and sweet especially when honey is added to it. It comes from the country of my favorite person :P

Firstly we will list all the required ingredients for this recipe:

  • 1 1/2 cups of semolina flour
  • 1/4 cup AP flour
  • 2 cups warm water
  • 2 tsp dry yeast
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • honey
  • melted butter

Instructions

  1. Put all of the ingredients in a blender except for the melted butter and honey and blend everything together till you have a consistent batter. Pour into a bowl and place a towel over it and let it sit for 15 mins in room temperature.
  2. Preheat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour roughly 1/4 cups of the batter in the pan and let it cook until you see holes emerge and the top looks dry, this usually takes 2-3 mins. DO NOT OVER COOK.
  4. Place done baghrir on a plate and pour a bit of melted butter.
  5. Serve warm and with honey on the side to drizzle on top if wish to do so.
  6. ENJOY!
