Fried Rice

This is a recipe to make basic fried rice, not the traditional Asian way but the lazy nigga way with the basic ingredients

Firstly we will list all the required ingredients for this recipe:

  • Day old rice
  • 2 Tbsp of soy sauce
  • Frozen carrots and peas
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat pan and add vegetable oil.
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan, add salt and pepper and cook eggs till nearly done.
  3. Add day old rice to the pan and start sauteing.
  4. Add soy sauce to the pan.
  5. After cooking the ingredients for 2-3 mins add the frozen veggies and cook for 3mins.
  6. Taste and add salt or pepper if needed.
  7. ENJOY!
